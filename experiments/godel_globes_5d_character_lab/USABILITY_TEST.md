# Two-minute retail usability test

Test five people who have not seen the research diagrams. Do not explain the five
dimensions before starting.

1. Ask the player to make the left wing more curious and less playful.
2. Ask them to make the right wing more protective and decisive.
3. Ask them to make the character more reflective.
4. Ask them to travel to any named form, change one trait, and return.
5. Ask what the map's distance means and whether they believe it shows literal
   model geometry.

Record task completion, first error, completion time, and the player's explanation.
The prototype passes when at least four of five players complete steps 1–4 within
two minutes and describe the map as approximate character similarity rather than
literal model structure. Collect preference comments separately; delight is not a
validity gate.
